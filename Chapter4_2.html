Characteristic equation

The characteristic equation of the n by n matrix A is A quantitative equation that contains useful information about the eigenvalues. The numerical equation det(A- λI) is called the characteristic equation of A, and λ is the eigenvalue of the matrix A if λ is the root of the characteristic equation det(A- λI)=0.

Solve the characteristic equation of matrix



Characteristic equation is:

(5-λ)^2*(3-λ)*(1-λ) = 0

Characteristic polynomial

det(A-λI) is called the characteristic polynomial of A.

Since the factor (5-λ) appears twice in the characteristic polynomial, the eigenvalue 5 has a multiplicity of 2. In general, the multiplicity of the eigenvalue as the root of the characteristic equation is called the λ multiplicity.
